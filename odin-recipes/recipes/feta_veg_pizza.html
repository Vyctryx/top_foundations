Feta & Veg Pizza

Description

This easy pizza creates a delicious feast suitable for vegetarians.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup brown rice
  • 3/4 cup vegetable stock
  • 1/2 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 brown onion diced
  • 100 g lamb mince
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1/2 zucchini grated
  • 1/2 cup kale roughly chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C on a fan forced setting.
  2. Lay the wrap flat on a plate and top with the tomato paste.
  3. Next, add the red onion, red capsicum and mushrooms.
  4. Crumble over the feta, drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Place the pizza in the oven for 10-15 minutes, until cooked to your liking.
  6. Remove from the oven and add the rocket on top.
